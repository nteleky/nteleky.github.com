About Me

I’m currently an undergraduate at Tufts University, Class of 2015. I’m majoring in Computer Science through the School of Engineering and minoring in Music.

My interests are pretty varied. As a CS major, I have an interest in web and mobile development. I’ve developed and contributed to several projects in web programming courses, including a Twitter-like message service, a parking application, and a Django plug-in to help developers enable rate-limiting easily. I have a decent background in C/C++, Java, and Javascript, and I’m currently learning Python and Ruby. I’m particularly fond of libraries and tools like jQuery, CoffeeScript, and SASS, because they can abstract away tedious code and let me focus on what I want to do, as opposed to how to write it.

Music is my other passion. I’m a piano player, focusing in jazz. I’ve been playing since I was 7, and currently play some gigs - usually at events like cocktail hours. I also accompany choirs, and work on practice tracks as required. Finally, I also arrange music, typically for high school or college vocalists (so far).

I spend pretty much the rest of my time doing mock trial, with Tufts Mock Trial. We’re one of the top ranked programs in the country, and we hope to keep that distinction going forward.
