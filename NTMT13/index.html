Hey TMT! I'm Nick Teleky, and I want to be your Co-President.

This last year has been historic for our program. We were the only team in the country to send four teams to regionals and get #4bids. For the first time in Tufts Mock Trial history, we managed to get #2bids to Nationals. We've seen unprecendented success .

But we can do better.

The challenge we face this year is to keep getting better at mock trial, but at the same time make sure it's fun. As co-president, my vision would be to expand on the foundation that's been laid by solving the problems we face and improving member satisfaction to truly make TMT one of the best mock programs in the country. By focusing on practical solutions and improving communication, we can get there. I am committed to make your voice heard and make our program better.
